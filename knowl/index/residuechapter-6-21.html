Exercise 21.

Give another proof of the Fundamental Theorem of Algebra (Theorem 5.3.2), using Rouché’s Theorem 9.3.3. (Hint: If \(p(z) = a_n z^n + a_{n-1} z^{n-1} + \dots + a_1 z + 1\text{,}\) let \(f(z) = a_n z^n\) and \(g(z) = a_{n-1} z^{n-1} + a_{n-2} z^{n-2} + \dots + a_1 z + 1\text{,}\) and choose as \(\gg\) a circle that is large enough to make the condition of Rouché’s theorem work. You might want to first apply Proposition 5.3.1 to \(g(z)\text{.}\))
