Definition 6.2.2.

Let \(G \subset \C\) be a region. The function \(u : G \to \R\) has a strong relative maximum at \(w \in G\) if there exists a disk \(D[w,r] \subseteq G\) such that \(u(z) \le u(w)\) for all \(z \in D[w,r]\) and \(u(z_0) \lt u(w)\) for some \(z_0 \in D[w,r]\text{.}\) The definition of a strong relative minimum is analogous.
