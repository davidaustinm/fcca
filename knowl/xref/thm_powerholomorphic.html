Theorem 8.1.1.

Suppose \(f(z) = \s c_k \left( z - z_0 \right)^k\) has radius of convergence \(R>0\text{.}\) Then \(f\) is holomorphic in \(D[z_0, R]\text{.}\)

Proof.

Corollary 7.4.9 says that \(f\) is continuous in \(D[z_0, R]\text{.}\) Given any closed piecewise smooth path \(\gamma \subset D[z_0, R]\text{,}\) Corollary 7.4.10 gives \(\int_\gg f = 0\text{.}\) Now apply Morera’s theorem (Corollary 5.2.1).
