Proposition 9.2.4.

  1. If \(z_0\) is a removable singularity of \(f\) then \(\ds \Res_{z=z_0}(f(z)) = 0\text{.}\)
  2. If \(z_0\) is a pole of \(f\) of order \(n\) then
    \begin{equation*} \Res_{z=z_0} (f(z)) \= \frac 1 {(n-1)!} \lim_{z \to z_0} \frac{ d^{n-1} }{ \diff{z}^{n-1} } \bigl( (z - z_0)^n f(z) \bigr) \, \text{.} \end{equation*}
