Exercise 9.

  1. Show that \(\frac 1 {k!} \le \frac 3 {k(k+1)}\) for any positive integer \(k\text{.}\)
  2. Conclude with Example 7.2.3 that for any positive integer \(n\text{,}\)
    \begin{equation*} 1 + \frac 1 2 + \frac 1 6 + \dots + \frac 1 {n!} \ \le \ 3 \,\text{.} \end{equation*}
