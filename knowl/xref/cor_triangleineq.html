Corollary 1.3.5.

For \(z_1, z_2, \dots, z_n \in \C\text{,}\) we have the following relations:
  1. The triangle inequality:
    \begin{equation*} \abs{\pm z_1\pm z_2} \le \abs{z_1}+\abs{z_2}\text{.} \end{equation*}
  2. The reverse triangle inequality:
    \begin{equation*} \abs{\pm z_1\pm z_2} \ge \bigl| \abs{z_1}-\abs{z_2} \bigr| \text{.} \end{equation*}
  3. The triangle inequality for sums:
    \begin{equation*} \abs{\sum_{k=1}^n z_k} \ \le \ \sum_{k=1}^n \abs{z_k} \text{.} \end{equation*}
